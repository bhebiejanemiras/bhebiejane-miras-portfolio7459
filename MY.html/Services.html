PROJECT 1

The Global Web

From Research Networks to Social Media

A presentation that highlights the pioneering individuals whose ideas and inventions shaped modern computing. It examines their key contributions, how their work directly connects to today’s IT tools and industries, and the lasting impact they left on our digital world. The project includes a PowerPoint presentation and a creative infographic imagining the next generation of future tech innovators. Includes a Presentation and an Infographic.

PROJECT 2

Accessibility for PWDs in the Digital Realm

A presentation that examines digital accessibility issues in the Philippines, focusing on legal requirements and the current compliance of websites and mobile apps. It includes a case study analyzing accessibility barriers faced by Persons with Disabilities (PWDs), a “what-if” scenario on the consequences of poor accessibility, and an infographic offering practical guidelines for developers to create more inclusive digital platforms.
Includes a Presentation and an Infographic.

PROJECT 3

IT Professional & Business Practice

Business Workflow Analysis, Solving Social & Technological Struggles and limitations Project

A group project where students act as IT consultants by interviewing a local business to identify a manual workflow that can be improved with a simple digital solution. The task includes documenting the current process, proposing an improved IT-based workflow, and presenting the findings. Deliverables are a semi-research paper, two flowcharts, and a presentation deck. This project allows students to apply real-world business analysis and IT skills.
Includes a Presentation and an Infographic.
