BHEBIE JANE C. MIRAS

Hi, I’m Jane! I may be new to the professional web design world, but I’m far from inexperienced. Through my IT studies, I’ve built a strong foundation in web development, design principles, and user-centered layouts. I’ve completed multiple academic projects where I created functional, visually cohesive websites—from planning and wireframing to coding and refining the final design.

These projects helped me develop an eye for detail, a clean design approach, and a problem-solving mindset. I enjoy turning ideas into smooth, responsive, and visually appealing web experiences. What I lack in industry years, I make up for with dedication, creativity, and a genuine passion for learning and improving.

I’m excited to bring my growing skills into real-world projects and collaborate with people who appreciate thoughtful design and fresh perspectives.
