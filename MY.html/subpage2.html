Accessibility for PWDs in the Digital Realm

A presentation that explores the challenges faced by Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) in accessing digital platforms in the Philippines. It discusses existing accessibility laws, evaluates how well popular websites and mobile apps comply with these standards, and presents a case study showing real barriers a PWD may encounter online. The project also includes a “what-if” scenario illustrating the potential consequences of poor accessibility, along with an infographic that provides practical, developer-friendly guidelines for creating more inclusive and accessible digital experiences.
