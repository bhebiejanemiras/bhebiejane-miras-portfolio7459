The Global Web

A presentation that highlights the pioneering individuals whose groundbreaking ideas and inventions laid the foundation for modern computing. It explores their major contributions, explains how their innovations directly influence the IT tools, software, and industries we rely on today, and illustrates the long-term impact of their vision on our digital world. The project features a PowerPoint presentation and a creative infographic that imagines the next generation of future tech innovators and the technologies they may develop.
