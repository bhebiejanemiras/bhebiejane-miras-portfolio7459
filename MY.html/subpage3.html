Business Workflow Analysis Project

A group project that simulates a real IT consulting engagement. Students will interview a local small or medium-sized business to identify a manual workflow that can be improved with a simple IT-based solution. The project involves documenting the current process, proposing an improved digital workflow, and presenting the findings professionally. Deliverables include a semi-research paper, two flowcharts (current and proposed workflows), and a presentation deck. Groups must also assign a leader and secretary to track meetings and participation. This project helps students apply IT and business analysis skills in a real-world context.
